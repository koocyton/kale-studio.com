How to sign in

  1. Open the LoomNote app on your phone
  2. Go to Settings or Scan to log in to Web
  3. Scan the QR code on the left to authorize

Security

  • Confirmed by the app — Web never stores your password
  • Notes are stored with end-to-end encryption
  • You can revoke Web access anytime in the app

Web features

  • Rich text editing with Markdown and images
  • Folders and full-text search
  • Real-time sync with your phone
